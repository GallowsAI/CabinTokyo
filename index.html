A Cozy Tokyo Bar Experience

Find a riverside spot in Nakameguro that offers whiskey from around the world and creative cocktails in a calm, rustic setting.

1. Discover a Welcoming Tokyo Bar

If you live in Tokyo or are simply exploring the city, you may know how special it feels to find a quiet haven in the midst of so much energy. At CABIN, we want to offer you that haven. We are in Nakameguro, which is one of the most scenic neighborhoods in the city. Our interior balances rustic design with modern accents. This creates a setting that is perfect for slow conversations and high-quality drinks.

When you step through our door, our team is glad to greet you. We guide you through our wide range of whiskeys, handcrafted cocktails, and Asian-inspired snacks that highlight seasonal flavors. We believe a bar is more than a place to drink. It is a space where people gather, share stories, and make lasting memories.

2. Meet Our Bartender and See the Menu

Every great Tokyo bar has a standout bartender. At CABIN, we are fortunate to have Sullivan Gouvea. He is from São Paulo in Brazil and has more than 25 years of food and beverage experience. He has mastered the art of classic cocktails and enjoys creating new ideas that captivate the senses. He chooses each ingredient with care and takes pride in how each drink looks and tastes.

Our menu has more than whiskey, though our Japanese and Scotch offerings are a highlight. You will also find craft beers, wines, and boutique liqueurs. Try our Whiskey Flights to see how different bottles can spark new tastes. If you enjoy cocktails, we invite you to sample our signature choices:

3. Ice That Elevates Each Drink

We pay attention to every detail, down to the type of ice we use. Good ice, cut or shaped with care, can be the difference between a normal drink and an extraordinary one. This short slideshow will show you a little of how we prepare these crystal-clear cubes.

Whether you choose a neat pour of a rare whiskey or our Old Fashioned, the right ice will help keep your drink perfect from start to finish. This extra care is part of our identity as a Tokyo bar that aims to give you the best possible experience.

4. Private Events and Contact Details

Are you planning a special event in Tokyo? Our cozy space can seat up to 20 guests. We can also host 30 people with partial seating. Picture a relaxed evening lit by gentle lights and made richer by a curated menu of cocktails and spirits. This can work well for birthdays, corporate gatherings, or small wedding receptions.

Call us or book online to learn more about hosting a private event at CABIN. We will work with you to create a personal experience that leaves everyone smiling. If you want a calm night with friends, a guided whiskey tasting, or a festive party, our Tokyo bar is ready to surpass your expectations.
